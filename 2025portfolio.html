ALL THAT GLITTERS, was production designed by me, as well as Darielle Campbell, Ben Howard, and Art Directed by Connor Johnson

The production design for this film is built on the character's worldview, she belives everything is perfect so it is. Fresh flowers decorate her house, pink decor and vintage furniture are everywhere. Everything is perfect and everything sparkles. Even blood is a beautiful sparkly pink.

Towards the end her worldview crumbles, she is thrown back into a harsh reality. Her home is not as beautiful as it once was, the decor is broken, torn, there are no flowers, no live plants, less pink, less glitter. And the blood is a terrible striking red.

For this film we built three sets, a Roof set, by building a fake wall and hanging a backdrop in a studio. Elaine's house, which was a completely redecorated AirBNB that actually had two looks, one for the pink world and one for the blue world. And finally the TV stage set, which was just a backdrop in a studio.

We won the award for "Oustanding Achievement in Production Design" at the Spring 2025 Community College of Aurora Student Show.

Lizzzard, written and directed by Kylee Hill, was production designed by me.

This movie is a love letter to a simpler time, the early 2000s. Doing this project we had to create a teenage girls bedroom in 2006. She's messy, alternative, and is ALL about her favorite bands.

Her life isn't perfect, and her biggest problems are homework and a curfew.

For this film we built three sets, a Roof set, by building a fake wall and hanging a backdrop in a studio.
